How it works

The confidence engine

Every target, scored across five dimensions of evidence.

Si-gul doesn't return a black-box number. Each candidate is decomposed into interpretable confidence layers — genetic association, clinical evidence, protein druggability, safety, and literature density — so your scientists can interrogate the reasoning, not just the result.
